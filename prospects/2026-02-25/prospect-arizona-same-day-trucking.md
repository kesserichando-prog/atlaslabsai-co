## Company Overview

Arizona Same Day Trucking provides urgent freight delivery services across the Phoenix area, Arizona, and six neighboring states (California, Utah, Colorado, Nevada, New Mexico, Texas). Their core promise is dispatching a truck within 2 hours of receiving a call, 24/7. Services include same-day truckload, dedicated truck (non-stop), dock freight, and TSA-certified freight for air cargo.

The company has built a strong reputation based on customer testimonials highlighting professionalism, reliability, communication, and on-time performance. They serve both residential and commercial clients across various industries.

## Current State & Pain Points

**Evidence:** Website promises "truck dispatched within 2 hours" - manual phone-based dispatch typical for regionals.

**Automation Opportunity:**
- Automated dispatch software with GPS integration
- Real-time driver location and availability
- Intelligent load-to-driver matching
- Automated dispatch confirmations
- Route optimization before dispatch

**Potential ROI:** 30-40% reduction in dispatcher labor, faster dispatch times, improved fleet utilization.
