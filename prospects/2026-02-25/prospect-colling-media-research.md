## Pain Point Indicators

**From Website and Positioning:**
1. **"Data-driven strategies"** - Implies significant time spent on analytics and reporting
2. **"Demand better results"** - Client expectations for performance and transparency
3. **Multiple platforms** - Cross-platform management complexity
4. **"Fill your pipeline"** - Lead generation focus requires tracking and attribution
5. **"Not buzzwords"** - Results orientation means measurement is critical
6. **Diverse client industries** - Different KPIs and reporting requirements per client

**Inferred Pain Points:**
- **Reporting Overload**: Weekly/monthly client reports take 5-10 hours/week per account manager
- **Platform Silos**: Google Ads, Facebook, LinkedIn don't talk to each other - manual consolidation
- **Communication Gaps**: Clients want real-time updates, manual processes create delay
- **Onboarding Friction**: New clients require significant setup work
- **Performance Tracking**: Manual monitoring of campaigns to identify optimization opportunities

## Competitive Landscape
