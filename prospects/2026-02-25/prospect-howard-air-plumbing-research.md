## Specific Automation Value Propositions

### Phoenix-Specific Summer Peak Use Case

**The Math:**
- Peak summer days (June-September): 120 days
- Each technician typically does 4-6 jobs per day during peak
- Automated dispatch could add 1-2 extra jobs per technician per day
- If 20 technicians: 20-40 extra jobs per day × $200-500 average job = $4,000-20,000 extra revenue per PEAK DAY
- Across 90 peak days: $360,000 - $1,800,000 additional summer revenue
- Automation cost: $80,000-$250,000
- **ROI could be 2x - 20x in first summer alone**

**This is the killer use case.**

### Emergency Service Differentiation

- 110°F heat + AC failure = dangerous health situation
- "We're on the way" automated notification vs. "We'll be there sometime today"
- Real-time technician tracking for customers
- Automated priority logic for emergencies vs. non-urgent
- Customer stress level significantly lower with good communication

### Maintenance Contract Foundation

- Recurring revenue is most valuable
- Automated annual scheduling for hundreds/thousands of contracts
- Automated renewal offers and reminders
- Current manual process creates significant revenue leakage
