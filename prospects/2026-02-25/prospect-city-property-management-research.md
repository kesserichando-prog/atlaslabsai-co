## Risk Factors & Considerations

### Positive Indicators
- ✅ 45+ year stable operation
- ✅ Massive scale (90,000 doors) = efficiency multiplier
- ✅ Existing technology investment (CITYCYNC®)
- ✅ Strong employee culture
- ✅ Capacity to invest ($8.5M revenue, 150+ employees)
- ✅ Clear pain point with customer communication

### Potential Objections/Risks
- ⚠️ "We already have CITYCYNC®" → Why add another system?
- ⚠️ Integration complexity → CITYCYNC® may be proprietary with limited APIs
- ⚠️ Company size = long sales cycle, multiple decision-makers
- ⚠️ "Homeowners hate HOAs anyway" → External factor beyond company control
- ⚠️ Family-owned → May be risk-averse to major changes

### Unique Opportunity
- Disconnect between employee experience (5-star) and customer experience (1-2 stars) = operational gap, not culture gap
- Fixable through automation and communication improvements
- Clear ROI: Improve customer reviews → attract more HOA clients
